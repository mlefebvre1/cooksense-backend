# Personas

## Primary — "Marc, the busy home cook"

| Trait               | Value                                                |
|---------------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| Age                 | 33                                                   |
| Family              | Partner + kids                                       |
| Tech savviness      | High (developer / tech worker)                       |
| Cooking level       | Advanced amateur                                     |
| Eating style        | Whole foods, low processed, health-conscious         |
| Cooking time at 6pm | ≤ 30 minutes                                         |
| Frustrations        | Ad-heavy recipe sites, long blog intros, repetition  |
| Goals               | Decide fast, eat well, learn the *why* behind dishes |

**Typical evening:**

> 5:45pm. Tired. Fridge has chicken thighs, half a cabbage, garlic, lemon.
> Picks up the phone, swipes 4 recipes in 20 seconds, taps one with the
> ingredients he has, starts cooking.

## Secondary — "Sophie, the curious learner"

| Trait          | Value                                                       |
|----------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|
| Age            | 28                                                          |
| Cooking level  | Intermediate                                                |
| Goal           | Get better. Understand techniques, not memorize 200 recipes |
| Frustrations   | Recipe sites assume zero skill or expert skill — no middle  |

She is the main consumer of the **Cooking School** content, and over time
graduates into a Marc-style user.

## Out of scope (not building for, MVP)

- Beginner cooks who need step-by-step videos.
- Restaurant professionals.
- Diet-specific users (keto, vegan, etc.) — they are welcome but not the
  optimization target.
